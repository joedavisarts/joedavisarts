T'Dane Gordon is a Jamaican content creator, pageant contestant, and digital storyteller with a passion for creating visually captivating content that connects authentically with audiences.

Her content brings lifestyle, beauty, fashion, travel, confidence, and empowerment together, anchored in high-quality production and intentional storytelling that allows brands to connect naturally through elevated, relatable experiences.

As a recent award-winning Miss Jamaica World contestant, she has built a growing platform centered around confidence, advocacy, and purposeful influence.

Author

T'Dane Gordon is the author of The Crowned Confidence Guide — a magazine-style guide for aspiring beauty queens and pageant contestants, covering everything they need to know before stepping onto the stage.

Spanning 8 steps across confidence, leadership, clarity, and queen energy, the guide covers pageant presence, Q&A mastery, personal branding, sponsorship, social media strategy, mindset, and advocacy — all through T'Dane's own journey and lens.

Available as both a digital e-book and a physical print edition.
