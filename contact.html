Before You Write

How quickly do you reply?

Within 24–48 hours on weekdays. Joe reads every message personally — no auto-responses, no assistants. If it's urgent, say so in the subject/first line.

Do I need to know my budget upfront?

A rough range helps, but it's not a dealbreaker. Joe uses Calcurate to scope projects transparently — you'll see exactly what you're paying for before any commitment.

Can you work internationally?

Yes — dual Jamaican-British citizenship means no visa complications for UK, EU, Caribbean, Canada, or US engagements. Travel logistics are straightforward and can be discussed as part of any booking.
