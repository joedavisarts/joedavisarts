FAQ

Do you work remotely or in-person only?

Both. Session work, production, and arrangement projects can be completed entirely remotely. Live performance and Music Direction are in-person — Joe travels internationally and holds dual citizenship for easy global access.

How do I get a quote?

Start with the contact form — describe your project, timeline, and rough budget. Joe uses Calcurate, a private scoping tool he built himself, to produce transparent, itemised quotes. No guesswork, no awkward conversations.

How far in advance should I book?

Every project is different — drop a message and we'll figure out what works. Generally the more lead time the better, especially for live shows and touring work.

Do you handle international travel logistics?

For international engagements, client-covered flights and accommodation are standard. Joe holds British-Jamaican dual citizenship — no visa complications for UK, EU, Caribbean, or North American work.
