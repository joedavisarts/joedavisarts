Joe Davis is a Jamaican-British musical director, arranger, and multi-instrumentalist whose work spans the full spectrum — from intimate studio sessions to stadium-scale live production. His ears hear in colour, his arrangements hit in the gut, and his bands always leave audiences asking for one more.

Rooted in the rich musical traditions of Jamaica and sharpened by international experience, Joe brings a rare combination of formal training, raw musicality, and bandleader instinct to every project he touches.
